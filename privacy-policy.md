# \# Privacy Policy for NewsLens

# 

# \*\*Last updated: March 31, 2026\*\*

# \*\*Developer: leozzz810\*\*

# 

# \## Overview

# 

# NewsLens is a Chrome extension that provides a personalized news dashboard with AI-powered summaries. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

# 

# \## Information We Collect

# 

# \### Information We Do NOT Collect

# \- We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII)

# \- We do not track your browsing history

# \- We do not sell or share your data with third parties

# \- We do not use cookies for tracking purposes

# 

# \### Information Stored Locally

# The following data is stored \*\*only in your browser\*\* using Chrome's local storage:

# \- Your bookmarked news articles

# \- Your preference settings (language, theme, categories)

# \- Cached news content for offline viewing

# 

# \### Information Sent to Our Server

# When you use the AI Q\&A feature, your query is sent to our backend API solely to generate a response. We do not log or store these queries.

# 

# \## How We Use Information

# 

# \- News content is fetched from public RSS feeds to display in your dashboard

# \- AI summaries are generated via our Cloudflare Worker backend

# \- All user preferences are stored locally on your device

# 

# \## Third-Party Services

# 

# NewsLens uses the following third-party services:

# \- \*\*Cloudflare Workers \& D1\*\* — backend API and database hosting

# \- \*\*Public RSS Feeds\*\* — news content sources

# 

# \## Data Retention

# 

# \- Local data (bookmarks, settings) remains on your device until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data

# \- Our server does not retain any personal data

# 

# \## Children's Privacy

# 

# NewsLens is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

# 

# \## Changes to This Policy

# 

# We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
